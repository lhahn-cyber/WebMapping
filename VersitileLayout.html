Washington DC

Founded in July 1790, Washington DC has a rich political history, and has many historical monuments and buildings, and is home to the federal government, home to the White House, Supreme Court, and the Capitol building.

But that's not all it has to offer, Washington DC is ripe with culture. Bars, music, and theatre are easy to find, and there are bike paths perfect for tourists or those who want to stay active.
